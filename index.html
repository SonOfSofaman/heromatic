Heromatic

Be a hero without all the bother of actual interactivity.

Download as .zip Download as .tar.gz View on GitHub

heromatic is a non-interactive RPG. Your hero will seek adventure in a procedurally generated world without any interaction from you. Your only input is an initial integer used to seed the mysterious forces of the universe.

So pick your favorite number, then sit back and watch as your hero triumphs or perishes at the hands of the RNG!
